Photographer

In December 2008 I had the privilege of using Nikon D60 for 3 hours. I had no clue that this short stint with DSLR would immediately hook me to photography and make it a passion for lifetime. The magic of experiencing the world from behind a pair of lenses completely amazed me. After that I bought a Canon RebelXs and started clicking pictures. I have come a long way from randomly clicked dark images to neatly composed and processed images, But there is so much more to learn and Quest. In my early days of being a Photographer I learned a lot from Ashwin and Rajith who practically mentored me. After that I started posting my photos on a flickr Group P@P. P@P is really a great group and organises amazing shoots. Now I am an active member of the group and was also part of Drushtikon 2010. I was also one of the photographers who covered a project called "1000 smiles" for Sakal (a regional popular News Media)
